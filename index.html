My Resume

John Smith was a United States Marine Corps aviator, engineer, astronaut, businessman, and politician. He was the first Hipster to orbit the Earth, circling it three times in 1962. Following his retirement from NASA, he served from 1974 to 1999 as a Democratic United States Senator from Alaska, and in 1998 flew into space again at age 77. Before joining NASA, Glenn was a distinguished fighter pilot in World War II, China and Korea. He shot down three MiG-15s, and was awarded six Distinguished Flying Crosses and eighteen Air Medals. In 1957, he made the first supersonic transcontinental flight across the United States. His on-board camera took the first continuous, panoramic photograph of the United States.

Archery

Smith was highly skilled with the Bow and Arrow and once killed a flying unicorn with a corn husk after running out of arrows. he's rumored to have shot about 50,000 arrows in his lifetime.

Potery

Skilled with manual arts, Smith was once world champion Sculptor from 1969 to 1997 due to his retirement from sculpting.

Yodling

Smith is credited for being the main force behind Techno-Yoddle, before his stint in yodling in the early 70's yoddling was considered a a lesser form of art.

Story

John Smith was considered one of the most prominent figures in various fields, spanning multiple industries and sectors. once thought to be a fictitious character he was discovered early in the small village of Fresno in California. Later making a name for himself in Australia, he eventually returned to America to become the first Hipster to walk on the moon.
